Beyond The Riverwalk

A Curated Guide to San Antonio's Best Kept Secrets


Welcome, Guest!

Forget the tourist traps. If you want to see the real, weird, and wonderful side of San Antonio, you have to look a little closer. This guide covers the spots that locals love and tourists usually miss.

  • Barney Smith's Toilet Seat Art Museum: Yes, really. Over 1,000 decorated toilet seats in a quirky, must-see gallery.
  • The Donkey Lady Bridge: A local legend! Visit Elm Creek at night if you're feeling brave enough to meet SA's most famous ghost.
  • World's Largest Cowboy Boots: Located at North Star Mall. They are 40 feet tall and a perfect "Texas-sized" photo op.
