Top Kid-Friendly Hidden Gems

Traveling with little ones? These spots offer plenty of space to explore without the crowds of the major theme parks:

  • Yanaguana Garden at Hemisfair: An incredible climbing structure and splash pad right in the heart of downtown.
  • The DoSeum Outdoor Yard: While the museum is popular, the outdoor "Big Outdoors" area is a shaded haven for active kids.
  • Pearsall Park: Offers the largest splash pad in the city and great hill views for a family sunset picnic.
  • Denman Estate Park: Features a beautiful Korean pagoda and a duck pond—perfect for a quiet morning walk.
