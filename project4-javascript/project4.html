LOCAL INSIGHT:
San Antonio winds can be unpredictable. Golden Hour is best for photos!
Wear comfortable shoes—the cobblestones around the Grotto are uneven.
The Japanese Tea Garden is most peaceful before 10:00 AM on weekdays.
Bar 1919 has a strict code of conduct for a sophisticated vibe.
